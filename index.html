How to join the closed test

如何加入封闭测试

1 Join the tester group 加入测试群组 Copy the Google Group link, open it in your browser with VPN, and join the tester group first. 先复制 Google Group 链接，再使用浏览器和 VPN 打开，并先加入测试群组。
2 Open the Google Play test link 打开 Google Play 测试链接 After joining the group, copy the Play link and open it in your browser. 加入群组后，再复制 Play 链接并在浏览器中打开。
3 Tap "Become a tester" 点击 “Become a tester” After the Play page opens, tap "Become a tester" before trying to install the app. 打开 Play 页面后，请先点击 “Become a tester”，再开始安装应用。
4 Install on Android and enjoy the app 在安卓上安装并开始体验 Install the app, then use it naturally to review Chinese, improve your HSK preparation, and help us improve it for the KUST community. 安装后就可以自然地用它练中文、提升 HSK 备考效果，也帮助我们把它做得更适合昆明理工大学国际学生使用。

FAQ

常见问题

Why do I need to join the group first? 为什么一定要先加入群组？ The Google Group controls who can access the closed test. Please join it before opening the Play testing link. Google Group 决定谁可以进入封闭测试，所以请先加入群组，再打开 Play 测试链接。
Why might the link not open in WeChat? 为什么微信里可能打不开链接？ WeChat does not open Google links reliably in China. If the page does not open, copy the link and use your browser with VPN. 在中国，微信通常无法稳定打开 Google 链接。如果页面打不开，请复制链接，并使用浏览器和 VPN。
Do I need a VPN? 我需要 VPN 吗？ Yes, in most cases you will need VPN access to open the Google Group and Google Play links from China. 大多数情况下需要。因为在中国访问 Google Group 和 Google Play 链接，通常都需要 VPN。
Can beginners use the app? 初学者可以使用吗？ Yes. The app covers multiple HSK levels and can be useful for both beginners and more advanced learners. 可以。这个应用覆盖多个 HSK 等级，对初学者和更高阶段的学习者都很有帮助。
Can iPhone users join? iPhone 用户可以参加吗？ Not in this closed test version. The current test is Android only. 当前这个封闭测试版本不可以，目前只支持安卓。
Do I need to tap "Become a tester"? 我需要点击 “Become a tester” 吗？ Yes. On the Google Play closed-test page, tap "Become a tester" before installing the app. 需要。在 Google Play 封闭测试页面中，请先点击 “Become a tester”，再安装应用。
